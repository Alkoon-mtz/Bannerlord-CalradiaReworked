Guides
If you want to use a piece for another weapon "type" and the vanilla game doesn't allow it, you have to add this piece to the weapon description (via xslt)
for example adding the `axe_craft_26_head` to throwing axe weapon.
`CraftingTemplate.ThrowingAxe` use 2 `WeaponDescription` and so you have to add your piece in both weapon description, in our case `ThrowingAxe` and `OneHandedAxe`
Since the `axe_craft_26_head` is already "allowed" in `OneHandedAxe` description+craftingtemplates you don't have to add it
But you have to add it in `ThrowingAxe` WeaponDescription.AvailablePieces and CraftingTemplate.UsablePieces
In `weapondescriptions.xslt` 
```xml
    <xsl:template match="WeaponDescription[@id='ThrowingAxe']/AvailablePieces">
        <xsl:copy>
            <xsl:apply-templates/>
            <AvailablePiece id="axe_craft_26_head" />
        </xsl:copy>
    </xsl:template>
```
and in `craftingtemplates.xslt`
```xml
    <xsl:template match="CraftingTemplate[@id='ThrowingAxe']/UsablePieces">
        <xsl:copy>
            <xsl:apply-templates/>
            <UsablePiece piece_id="axe_craft_26_head" />
        </xsl:copy>
    </xsl:template>
```
